Directories are where files are stored in either your computer or another site such as Github. Directories in of themselves are files that store other files creating a tree in the system. 
VCS helps track changes, which shows a process of how each change was done. They can also revert back to a previous version if the changes didn't seem to work in their favor. It can be shared and collaborated among other parties.
