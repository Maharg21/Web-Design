At the Mountains of Madness

Howard Phillips Lovecraft

At the Mountains of Madness is a sci-fi cosmic horror novella about a disasterous expedition to Antarctica. From a short synopsis, the story is told by a series of events yet to be described by a geologist to dissuade other group of explorers from exploring the continent.

The events are described by the findings of an ancient civilization that have been there longer than humanity and finding untold stories of the Earth's past through murals, until disaster inevitably strikes.
